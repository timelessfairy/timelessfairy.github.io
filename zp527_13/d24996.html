Our Democratic First Amendment

著者名: Ashutosh Bhagwat 出版者: Cambridge University Press 出版年: 04 Jun 2020 版表示: シリーズ: HardbackReaders interested in constitutional history, law, and politics will enjoy this engaging examination of the origins of the First Amendment and its relationship to democracy. Illuminating lessons from the conflicts of the Framing era, Bhagwat provides a deeper context for understanding contemporary politics in the age of social media.
