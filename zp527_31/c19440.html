    How To Pair/Reset K380S Microphone with Receiver?
    1. Power off both Mic & Receiver. Firstly, hold press the PAIR button in Mic tail, then hold press Mic"s Power Key until you see"PAIR" flashes. This means Mic has entered Pairing mode. Another mic is the same procedure.
    2. Long press Receiver"s Power Key, you will notice RX MIC A/B Indicator will quickly flash 3 times then out. This means RX has detected Mic A/B.
    3. Now short press Mic"s Power Key to comfirm pairing. If Receiver"s RX MIC A/B Indicator always on, that means Mic A/B & Receiver pairing successful. Otherwise, repeat the whole pairing process.
