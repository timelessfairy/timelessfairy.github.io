120V 13A EGO Energy Regulator 50.57131.010 Stepless Regulating Power

EGO energy regulator can realize the infinite adjustment between 6% and 70% at the rated power. It can continue heating at the highest set,its output power is 100% of the rated output. It can achieve high power output through long circuit current and short break circuit,just as heating and fried.It can achieve low power output through short circuit current and long break circuit, just as slow cooking.
