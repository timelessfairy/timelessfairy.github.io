    User Manual:
    1. Power Bluetooth battery with adapter (The red light is on - it is charging)
    2. Set the communication parameters of the total station through menu
    Baud rate: 9600ps
    Check/Parity: None
    Data bits: 8 / Serial Port: COM 8
    Stop bit: 1
    ACK: Standard
    CR, LF: Open
    Record type： REC-A
    3. Insert the connector of the Bluetooth adapter into the serial port of the total station and turn on the Bluetooth switch.
    4. Open the surveyor APP on the mobile phone, click Bluetooth, select the instrument model to be topcon1, serial port delay 0.5 seconds, click scan, a new device appears: BT18, select it, enter the pairing password 1234
    5. Connect to Bluetooth, click test
    Note：
    The blue light flashes - Bluetooth is not connected, blue light
    The blue light is always on - Bluetooth is successfully connected
