説明: GRONGU Air Blower Seal Repair Kit Compatible with Coleman SaluSpa Replacement for Saluspa Replacement Parts

Replacement for Saluspa replacement parts designed for fix water leaks related to the air bubbler/blower for Coleman SaluSpa AirJet hot tubs.

Compatibility
The seal repair kit compatible with SaluSpa/Lay-Z-Spa AirJet model 54112, 54124, 54129, 54131, 54145, 54155, 54176, 54185, 54186, 54190, 54295, 54297, 90443, 90437, 90363, 13804, 600xx, and many other variants.
Note:The full set of silicome seals not compatible with HydroJet or HydroForce models.Compatible with both 2nd generation (egg shaped) and 3rd generation (square shaped) heater/pump units.

The water pump seal repair kit Include
1 × umbrella valve (with retrofit plastic housing that installs at the front of the air inlet tube)
1 × air inlet seal (connector “A”)
2 × dome/mushroom seals
2 × large o-rings
1 × small o-ring (applicable only to older 2nd gen pumps)

Important:
The umbrella valve housing must be secured to the air inlet tube by connecting the heater/pump unit on top of it. If the housing is not secured and left unattended, it could become dislodged by water pressure and flood the area.
OEM designed the umbrella valve to reduce the flow of water to a slow drip or trickle. But even with the factory-installed valve on a brand new spa, there will be a small amount of leaking. This is normal.
That is why there are additional seals between the liner and the pump and inside the pump, and a cap for when the pump is disconnected.
These are not OEM, these are quality GRONGU brand replacement parts.
