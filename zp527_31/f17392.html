Introduction:
JF-10 Dynamic Compressor guitar effect pedal features a re-creation of the classic ross compressor with very low noise.
It has three adjustment knobs, "sustain", "attack" and "level". The "attack" adjustment is designed for bass or high-output active pickups to better suit the instruments.

With the three controls of Sustain, Attack & Level you can easily dial in your perfect levels of compression. Whether you just need it to keep the volumes even on clean passages and to stop any notes overpowering others or whether you want to use it after your overdrive pedals to give you near endless sustain, it"s up to you. You could even use it as a solo boost at the very end of your chain by setting the Sustain & Attack controls to taste and then setting the Level control so it"s louder when it"s turned on.

A lot of players don"t think they"ll need a compressor or when they first use one don"t think it"s actually doing anything. Once you realise when and where to use a compressor then it becomes an essential part of your tone. It means you can control when and how much sustain you can have on certain passages. A compressor allows you to play delicately picked passages with masses of effects and you"ll hear every note perfectly without any of them disappearing into the ether or overpowering the others.
